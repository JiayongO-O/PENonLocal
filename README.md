## Photon-Efficient Imaging


#### 1. Overview

The implementations are for the photon-efficient imaging in following papers:

Jiayong Peng, Zhiwei Xiong, Xin Huang, Zheng-Ping Li, Dong Liu, Feihu Xu. "[Photon-Efficient 3D Imaging with A Non-Local Neural Network](https://www.ecva.net/papers/eccv_2020/papers_ECCV/html/5471_ECCV_2020_paper.php)". In ECCV 2020.

Jiayong Peng, Zhiwei Xiong, Hao Tan, Xin Huang, Zheng-Ping Li, Feihu Xu. "[Boosting Photon-Efficient Image Reconstruction With A Unified Deep Neural Network](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/9864284)". TPAMI 2022.
